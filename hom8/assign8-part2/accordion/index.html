Register for Classes ¹

Steps to Register: View your registration date and time, fee payment information, and registration holds through "My Registration" in your SDSU WebPortal. If you have not yet created a portal account, follow the instructions on this page. You must have a working email address to activate an account. Pay your fees by your fee payment deadline and clear any registration holds shown. Details are provided at "My Registration." Plan your class schedule by using the "My Planner" function in WebPortal Use the registration tools through "My Registration" in the WebPortal to register online at your assigned date and time. Pay attention to the registration limits.
